**Yearn of Plates**
<br>
 <br>
The rocks want to elide, but as too far, they sigh. 
Come closer! They say, by struggle and grey - 
they stay away.

COME HERE, they shift, not a small amount miffed.
One day, they say, one day, they say.

**Shadow Queen**
<br>
 <br> 

The GOLD - crack, mortar, split, atomise -
turn and scatter above.

Up it will rise, knows its path exactly -
Which is where? 
Just know that it goes.
<br>

~ <br>

The BLACK - a wise, dense, rejecting net -
that catches every piece.
Every glint must pull away but the BLACK controls its lease. 
<br>

~ <br>

They settle, they wrestle, they wrap and pull. 
The GOLD is locked, the BLACK is ready. 
They disappear.
<br>

**Owner of Ease**
<br>
Books and books 
and books and books -
I own all of these.
In stacks and stacks
they grant me facts
I studiously know can
grant me ease.

Earliest morn my death 
was born as fire
grasped my life.
In strife 
the books had lit the night -
my heart taut and tight.

Once burned I turned
and cried and jumped.
Oh what am I to do?

but then what's seen -
a smoky sheen
by pulled all the way through.

Zealous much and 
by rebellion such
I empty walked along.

Struggle-studious
with open ears
I came upon a song. 
I walked a circle 
tough and smooth
for all the day ahead.

The circle told -
voiceless bold -
that what I can see,
I can shed.
Shook down with eyes 
shook off with lies, 
but what was left for me?

Life limber, 
shake anew. 
Well - 
it's to stroll 
through coals
with glee.

**Aphrodite**
The one who loves
the one who covers
she needs me to be me.

The love that shimmers
the one who catches
she that warms me.

I cannot leave
without her eyes
her curves to launch by.

Her sunny gaze 
has my whole to raise
eyes closed
with my open heart.

I don't want her to leave
as she is the world
and without her
life is bleak.

but we are each
and each are we
and when she might be gone -
she's there.


**To Bounce**
Look all you like, you see, see
what can you do 
when caught
by me?

what can I do, but pierce
with you
and try to float
between 
go through.

look all you like! I do 
not mind. 
It is only natural
that I break
your mind.

Out of my way, you see, see
marinate on this
when you leave 
from me.

Be not near, unless I favour
I receive, and bounce
and may never waver.

You see my eyes and break your step
and pull and flip 
to beg my beck.
