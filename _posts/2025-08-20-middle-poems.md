**Transcendentaland**

The vile brand
of Nick Land
Ick Land
transcendentaland

Scared of limits
ontophilic
Sick Land 
transcendentaland

On King's Bible stakes
groundless libel takes
Quick Land to
transcendentaland

as by Kant - he's trapped
and feels he cannot stand
his words hairdried sand in
transcendentaland

The ontophile self-spreads
lies and bedrugged dread
cries and shiny whys in
transcendentaland

Try and escape!
Another book bake!
Noumenal dreams be fake in
transcendentaland.


**'Messi, Soccer Sage'**

Messi, Soccer Sage -
Federer channeling Tennis' page.
These men who dance 
and may win and fast
should teach an academy
how to beat their class.


**Heaven's Carpets**

Shivers, no words,
you I saw and heard;
heavens shout about carpets rolled out
to see you clear is to be blinded joyful.


**Divinity in Retrograde**

We in the time of Thutmose knew, 
trapped in entangled abode.

Temple politics led life bereft, 
details for time to know.

Lived murder and torment and prayer and wrath, 
powers gelded and thrown.

Parted for millennia and tumbled thus,
pendulum bashed below.

By hook to climb we had to see,
and fallen parts pulled back to we. 

Shined smooth of juts of filth,
taught hard that wrongs do spill.



**Stop**

Stop all signals
every energetic lump.

All each corridor  
in where a sorry bump.

Stand focused fixed 
a swaying stump.

Receive, believe,
stop the world, 
clear the dump.


**Shadow Queen**

The GOLD - crack, mortar, split, atomise -
turn and scatter above.

Up it will rise, knows its path exactly -
Which is where? 
Just know that it goes.

~

The BLACK - a wise, dense, rejecting net -
that catches every piece.
Every glint must pull away but the BLACK controls its lease. 

~

They settle, they wrestle, they wrap and pull. 
The GOLD is locked, the BLACK is ready. 
They disappear.

~

A sailor pulls his oar, sliding through the slip - 
he must ascend to his abode
before he gets some lip.

Up he looks and pulls and tugs, 
going on his way.
And above the sky will guide -
'twould never make him pay.

**Rest Bringer**

Vesta, Oh Vesta, 
of Sacred Rest.

Bless my home
from North
to West.

Let family proudly
bear our crest

And thrive today in
pleasant nest.
